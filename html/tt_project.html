TaskTrick

Overview/Summary

Abstract: TaskTrick is an app designed to help students keep track of their tasks, improve productivity, and reduce procrastination. The app includes features such as task categorization, deadline reminders, and progress tracking.
Timeline: 4 months
Tools Used: Figma, ChatGPT, Google

Definition

- Find out everyday struggles and create an app that helps solve this issue.
- Aim for simplicity to engage a broader audience.
- Focus on making children want to learn responsibly.

Research

Methods: User interviews, surveys, and competitor analysis. We gathered insights from potential users to understand their pain points and preferences.
Timeline: 3 weeks
